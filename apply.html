Nominate a speaker

The Global Seminar provides a platform for emerging researchers to showcase their work in reticular chemistry. We invite direct applications from researchers and recommendations from mentors who wish to highlight exceptional talent.

We are actively building our schedule for the upcoming academic cycles:

Nominate a Speaker

We invite faculty and senior investigators to recommend emerging scholars who have demonstrated significant contributions to the field of Chemistry.

Upon submission, the Seminar Manager will coordinate with the nominee regarding scheduling and technical requirements.

Submit Nomination

Selection Process

Nominations are processed through the following review cycle:

01

Initial Nomination

Faculty or mentors submit a nomination via the form.

02

Speaker Application

Nominees are contacted directly to provide their full profile and research abstract.

03

Committee Review

The selection committee evaluates submissions on a monthly rolling basis.

04

Formal Invitation

Selected speakers receive a formal invitation and scheduling details 8 weeks prior to their session.

Selection Criteria

Our committee evaluates submissions to ensure a diverse balance of research topics, institutional backgrounds, and global representation.

Once a submission is reviewed, the Seminar Manager will follow up via email with a formal invitation, time confirmation, and session logistics.
