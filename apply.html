Apply to present your research.

We welcome speakers across career stages (undergrad → postdoc) working in reticular chemistry and related porous materials.

What to prepare

  • Your name, affiliation, position
  • Lab / PI name
  • Talk title + abstract (150–250 words)
  • Preferred month(s) and your time zone
  • (Optional) Website / Google Scholar, headshot

Review process

We schedule talks to balance topics, time zones, and speaker diversity.

If selected, we’ll follow up by email with time confirmation and Zoom logistics.
