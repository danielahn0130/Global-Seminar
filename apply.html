Submission Guidelines

To ensure a smooth review process, please have the following ready for your application:

  • Institutional affiliation and current research position
  • Principal Investigator (PI) or Laboratory name
  • Technical title and abstract (150–250 words)
  • Preferred month(s) and time zone (Atlantic or Pacific)
  • Optional: Graphical abstract or 1-slide summary

Selection Criteria

Our committee evaluates submissions to ensure a diverse balance of research topics, institutional backgrounds, and global representation.

Once a submission is reviewed, the Seminar Manager will follow up via email with a formal invitation, time confirmation, and session logistics.
