Global Research Seminar in Reticular Chemistry

Global Seminar is a community-driven forum bringing together researchers in reticular chemistry and related fields to share work, exchange ideas, and support emerging scholars. By hosting bi-monthly sessions that tailored to global time zones, we ensure that high-impact scientific exchange is accessible to the international community, regardless of geography.
