Our Mission

Program Mission and Scope

Global Seminar is an international forum dedicated to researchers who drive the field of framework materials forward. We believe geographical distance shouldn’t limit scientific visibility. By bridging the gaps between research hubs, we provide a platform for scientists—regardless of their location or institution—to share their work with a worldwide audience.

Unified Global Exchange

Each 60-minute session features a 30–40 minute technical talk followed by an open moderated discussion.

We prioritize clarity and foundational motivation, ensuring that every presentation is accessible to students while remaining scientifically rigorous for experts.

Bridges Across Borders

Connecting the Americas, Europe, and the Asia-Pacific through coordinated monthly sessions.

By rotating session times between 04:00 UTC and 17:00 UTC, we ensure that researchers from all major time zones can engage in a single, unified community dialogue.
