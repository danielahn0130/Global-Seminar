Seminar Mission

Global Seminar is an international platform dedicated to the researchers and emerging scholars driving reticular chemistry and related fields forward. We believe that scientific exchange should be accessible, global, and community-driven. By bridging the distance between research hubs, we provide a forum for scientists—regardless of location—to share their work and strengthen connections between research communities worldwide.

Unified Global Exchange

Each 1 hour session features a 30 minute scientific presentation followed by an open discussion period.

We prioritize clarity and foundational motivation, ensuring that every presentation is accessible to students while remaining scientifically rigorous.

Bridges Across Borders

Connecting the Americas, Europe, Africa, and the Asia-Pacific through coordinated bi-monthly sessions.

By rotating session times between 04:00 UTC and 17:00 UTC, we ensure that researchers from all major time zones can engage in a single, unified community dialogue.
